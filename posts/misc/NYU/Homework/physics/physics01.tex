\question $\vec{A} \cdot \vec{B} = -1, |\vec{A} \times \vec{B}| = 17$

$$
\vec{A} \cdot \vec{B} = (5, 2) \cdot (1, -3) = 5 \times 1 + 2 \times (-3) = -1
$$
$$
|\vec{A} \times \vec{B}| = |(5, 2) \times (1, -3)| = |5 \times (-3) - 2 \times 1| = 17
$$
